About RV Stays

Park your RV and stay awhile on our peaceful 16-acre farm. We offer short- and long-term RV spaces surrounded by nature—near creeks, trees, or our quiet pond. Enjoy access to fresh air, water, community, and optional farm activities. Whether you're passing through or putting down roots, JAM Mission is your home on the road.

What's Included

  • Short-term and long-term RV spaces
  • Near creeks, trees, and pond
  • Access to water and community
  • Optional farm activities and events
