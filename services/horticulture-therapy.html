About Horticulture Therapy

Reconnect with the healing power of nature through hands-on gardening experiences. Our horticulture therapy program invites guests to plant, nurture, and harvest in a peaceful farm setting. Whether you're managing stress, trauma, or simply seeking calm, working with the earth promotes healing, mindfulness, and personal growth.

What's Included

  • Hands-on planting, nurturing, and harvesting
  • Peaceful farm garden setting
  • Promotes healing, mindfulness, and growth
  • Suitable for individuals and groups
