About Petting Zoo

Come meet our friendly goats, sheep, turkeys, guinea fowl, and chickens up close. Our petting zoo gives visitors of all ages a hands-on experience with farm animals in a safe, welcoming environment. Learn about each animal’s personality, help with feeding, and take home unforgettable memories.

What’s Included

  • Hands-on time with goats, sheep, turkeys, and chickens
  • Feeding and grooming stations
  • Educational talks about animal care and farm life
  • Hand-washing and sanitizing stations throughout
