Farm Animal Tours

About Farm Animal Tours

Come visit our peaceful 16-acre refuge and meet the animals that make JAM Mission special! Walk among goats, sheep, chickens, turkeys, geese, and more while learning how we care for them and how they help support the land and community. Guests receive free fresh farm eggs when available—a small gift from our happy hens!

What's Included

  • Guided tour of our 16-acre farm
  • Meet goats, sheep, chickens, turkeys, and geese
  • Learn about animal care and sustainable farming
  • Free fresh farm eggs when available
