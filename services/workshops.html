Workshops & Learning

About Workshops & Learning

Join our hands-on workshops focused on sustainability, farming, wellness, and self-sufficiency. From animal care to renewable energy, our programs empower you with practical knowledge and real-world skills.

Our Workshop Programs

Animal Care

Basic care for farm animals, feeding, grooming, building enclosures, animal behavior

Sustainable Farming

Organic gardening, composting, crop rotation, soil health, planting seeds, creating food forests

Homesteading Skills

Self-sufficiency through canning, soap-making, candle-making, water management, herbal remedies, natural pest control

Nature-Based Arts & Crafts

Weaving with natural fibers, building birdhouses, decorations from natural materials, crafting with reclaimed wood

Wellness in Nature

Time with God sessions, herbal remedy, healthy meal plans, journaling, gratitude jars

Farm-to-Table Cooking

Harvest ingredients, prepare a meal, shared feast, fermented juice making

Leadership & Team-Building

Group projects like building fences, coops, garden structures, raised beds, irrigation systems

DIY Renewable Energy

Build small solar panels, wind energy, DIY water collection systems

Zero-Waste Living

Reusable beeswax wraps, upcycling old materials, reducing food waste

Herbal Medicine & Gardening

Growing medicinal plants, herbal teas, salves, essential oils, tinctures
