About Nature Tours

Explore the natural beauty surrounding Jam Mission with our guided nature tours. Walk through wooded trails, discover local plant and animal species, and learn about sustainable land management. Our tours are suitable for all fitness levels and offer a peaceful way to connect with the land.

What’s Included

  • Guided walks led by experienced naturalists
  • Wildlife spotting and plant identification
  • Lessons on sustainable land management
  • Suitable for all ages and fitness levels
