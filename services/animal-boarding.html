About Animal Boarding

Need a safe, caring place for your animals while you're away? JAM Mission offers spacious, secure, and loving animal boarding on our 16-acre farm. With daily care, fresh air, and plenty of space to roam, your livestock or pet will feel right at home. We treat every guest like family—because they are.

What's Included

  • Spacious, secure enclosures on 16 acres
  • Daily feeding, care, and monitoring
  • Fresh air and room to roam
  • Livestock and pet-friendly
