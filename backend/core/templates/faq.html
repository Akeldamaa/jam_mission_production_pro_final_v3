Frequently Asked Questions

What is JAM Mission?

JAM Mission is a faith-driven, self-sufficient community dedicated to providing shelter, food and resources to those in need while promoting a deeper connection with nature. We offer unique experiences such as a mobile petting zoo, RV stays, camping and hands-on workshops to educate, inspire and support sustainable living. Our purpose is to inspire healing, growth and second chances by fostering meaningful connections with nature, community and our Creator.

How do I book a stay or an event?

Visit the Events page to see upcoming events or head to our Book Your Stay page to request a campsite or RV spot. You can also inquire about services via our contact page.

How do I pay for bookings or orders?

After submitting a booking or order form, please send payment via Zelle or Venmo to thejammission@gmail.com. Once payment is received, we will confirm your reservation or purchase.

What products and services do you offer?

We offer fresh eggs, chicks, plants and other farm products. Our services include a mobile petting zoo, farm animal tours, animal therapy and boarding. You can browse our offerings on the Products and Services pages.

How can I contact you?

You can reach us by email at thejammission@gmail.com or by phone at 844‑238‑1848. Our mailing address is Tuskegee, Alabama. For specific enquiries, please use the contact page and we will respond as soon as possible.
